Our process

With over 15 years of experience, we've developed a method of building websites that yields success. We do customize the process for unique situations and projects but this is the general flow.

Information Gathering

We always start by gathering information about your project - what information and materials are available, what are the key deadlines and goals, etc. We put a schedule and project plan together.

UX Research & Design

It is very important to think through a website and tailor its structure, flow, and content towards your goal for the website and your customers goal in visiting. Our UX team will conduct research, perform user testing and assemble wireframes.

UI Design

Once the UX is complete our designers will design a Ul to fit the wireframes. They'll use photography, illustration, and modern design elements to create a design that will fit your website's purpose and goal.

Front-End Build

Our front-end developers will take the UI design and implement it into desktop and mobile responsive code that is compatible and tested with different browsers and devices. They'll also make sure your website is SEO optimized and loads fast.

Back-End Build

The back-end developers will take the front-end code and tie it into databases, create a back-end management system, tie your website into mailing software, ERPs, CMSs, and other third party software products that you use.

Testing & Launch

It's critical to perform quality control on a website before and after it launches. Our QC team will review your website to make sure its all functional and doesn't have any visual or compatibility issues. Once we're sure we'll launch it.

The Result

We offer a 30 day guarantee on all our websites to ensure that everything is working without bugs/issues. We also offer ongoing maintenance services as well as a high level consultative retainer support service. Ask about our ongoing consulting review services where well make suggestions on how to improve your website.
